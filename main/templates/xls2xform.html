XLS2XForm Converter

If you're looking for the XLS2XForm converter you're in the right place. We've incorporated the XLS2XForm converter into formhub.

To use it simply:

  • Signup for a free account with formhub
  • Upload your XLSForm to formhub and it'll instantly be converted to an XForm which will be available on your phone.
  • If you want to download the XForm, click on the form name and click on other to download the converted XForm
